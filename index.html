About Us

At Construction Cleaning Pros, we specialize in post-construction cleaning for both commercial and residential projects. Our dedicated team utilizes industry-leading techniques to eliminate debris, dust, and residues, ensuring your space is pristine and ready for occupancy.

Our Services

Debris & Dust Removal

After construction, dust and debris can create a hazardous and messy environment. Our team at Construction Cleaning Pros ensures every inch of your site is cleared of leftover materials, drywall dust, and other construction remnants. We use high-powered vacuums and professional cleaning techniques to make your space safe and spotless.

Window & Glass Cleaning

Construction often leaves windows covered in dust, paint splatters, and adhesive residues. Our streak-free window cleaning service restores clarity and shine to all glass surfaces, enhancing the overall appearance of your property. Whether it's high-rise windows or storefront glass, we have the tools and expertise to make them crystal clear.

Floor Cleaning & Polishing

Floors take the brunt of post-construction mess, with paint drips, dust, and grime accumulating quickly. We deep clean, scrub, and polish all flooring types, from tile to hardwood, ensuring a flawless finish. Our expert cleaning restores the original shine and removes any signs of construction work.

Deep Sanitization & Disinfection

Post-construction dust and debris can leave harmful particles in the air and on surfaces. We go beyond simple cleaning by fully sanitizing and disinfecting your space. Using hospital-grade and eco-friendly disinfectants, we eliminate germs, bacteria, and construction contaminants, making the area safe for employees, residents, or customers.

Pressure Washing

Exterior surfaces also need attention after construction! Our professional pressure washing services remove dirt, mud, and residue from sidewalks, driveways, and building exteriors. This final touch gives your property a fresh and polished look, ensuring a clean and welcoming appearance.
